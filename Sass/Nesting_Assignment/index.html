Frequently Asked Qustions

Who is a Creator?

A creator is someone who has a Bold BI account, and
creates, edits, shares or otherwise manages dashboards.
Only creators are counted

Who is a User?

A user is someone who has a Bold BI account, and can
securely view dashboards that others have created.Users
can interact with a dashboard using tools such as filter,
exporter, etc. that ate provided as a part of the
dashboard. Users are not counted for licensing purposes.

Is there a trial periods?

Every plan comes with a 15-day trial period during which
your card will not be charged.

Is there a contract period?

No, you may cancel your service at any time. You can
cancel online or by contracting the support team.

Can I move between plans?

Absolutely. You can move up or downs as your business
needs change.

Will my subscription be renewed automatically?

Yes, your sunscription will be automatically renewed each
month.
